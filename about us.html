HISTORY

Triple “A” Bakery was a bakery company established in 21st of January 2018. It was founded by three people that is Siti Nur Athirah binti Abu Amin, Muammar bin Akbal and Muhammad Aliff Iman bin Mohd Kholifah. It was named “ Triple “A” “ in conjunction with the names of the three owners of this bakery that is Ammar, Athirah and Aliff. The business of this bakery was being conducted in Malaysia.

At first, Athirah, Ammar and Aliff just only do the small business of the bread, cakes and pastries products and the business purposes is only for their additional earning because three of them have their own works. Three of them have their own skills and knowledge in making each of these three products. Athirah know how to make a variety types of cakes, Ammar know how to make a variety types of breads and Aliff know how to make a variety types of pastries. So it is easier for them to run their small business without needs to require the workers. But, after they have get so many orders from the customers, they’ve decided to open the bakery and quit their jobs. It is because they can’t handle the business with so many orders while working. So on 10th June 2021 is the date which they’ve started their bakery business. They’ve also have hired 10 workers for their bakery.

LOCATION

The headquarters of Triple “A” Bakery was located in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Besides their HQ, there was their bakery that selling their products. The buildings of HQ and bakery of Triple “A” Bakery was started being built on 15th February 2018 and the buildings was completed on 27th May 2021. The bakery was started being operated on 10th June 2021. The bakery was being builded in Kuala Lumpur because it was the stratergic location for a bakery which Kuala Lumpur is the capital of Ma laysia and it also the city of attraction in Malaysia. So, there will be many peoples not only Malaysian but also peoples from overseas especially those who love the bakery products. Kuala Lumpur also the workplace for many peoples. So, for those who want to eat quickly can just go to this backery and buy the products from this bakery in a short time.

PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

For the products that was being sold in the bakery, there were 3 types of bakery products that are breads, cakes and pastries. Three owners of the bakery know how to make these three types of products based on their knowledge and experience. There were a variety types of bread cakes and pastries with different types of flavors either it was sweet and spicy. There were also some drinks that were being sold by Triple “A” Bakery. There were 3 option for the customers to trying the products in Triple “A” Bakery. The options is they can dine-in at the bakery or they can choose for the packing of their products or they can get their products through the delivery. For the delivery, they were two types of delivery. First, the delivery from the Triple “A” Bakery. Second, the delivery from the Grabfood. They just need to order and pay online and just wait in front of their door until their foods arrived.
